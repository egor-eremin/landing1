CySec

The Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission is a popular licensing organization from Cyprus among brokers

ASIC

The Australian Securities and Investments Commission, which controls all financial markets in Australia and the world, including Forex

IFSC

IFSC (International Financial Services Commission of Belize) refers to the regulatory body of Belize, the main purpose of which is licensing and verifying the implementation of the rules

VFSC

The Vanuatu Financial Services Commission formally controls the financial markets of the island of Vanuatu and other regions

FSC Mauritius

The Vanuatu Financial Services Commission formally controls the financial markets of the island of Vanuatu and other regions

Morgan Stanley

One of the largest bank holdings in the world, which is a liquidity provider for Forex companies

UBS

A Swiss global bank that is an international financial partner of many companies and organizations, including Forex brokers

MiFID

MiFID is a Directive of the European Union on the markets of financial instruments regulating the activities of their participants
